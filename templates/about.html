About Todo App

Welcome to the Todo App! This simple web application allows you to manage your tasks and stay organized. Keep track of your to-do list, set priorities, and manage deadlines effectively.

Features of Todo App:

  • Add new tasks with a title, description, and due date.
  • Authentication with Login and Register.
  • Set a priority for tasks.
  • Edit or delete tasks as needed.
