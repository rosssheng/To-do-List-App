About Todo App

Welcome to the Todo App! This simple web application allows you to manage your tasks and stay organized. Keep track of your to-do list, set priorities, and manage deadlines effectively.

Features of Todo App:

  • Add new tasks with a title, description, and due date.
  • Set a priority for tasks.
  • Edit or delete tasks as needed.

Our mission is to provide a simple and efficient tool to help you manage your daily tasks and boost productivity. The Todo App is designed to be user-friendly and accessible on various devices, so you can stay organized anytime, anywhere.

If you have any feedback, questions, or suggestions, please feel free to contact us at ross62@bu.edu. We value your input and are always looking to improve the app based on user needs.
